About Me

Hello, world! I am a UI/UX Designer originally from Omaha, Nebraska, where I studied journalism and media communication. I worked at a local television news station for nearly two years before pursuing a master's degree in computer and information technology at the University of Pennsylvania and working as a front-end dev.

My work in design marries the curious, empathetic, research-driven heart of a journalist with the highly technical, innovative spirit of a geeky coder.

For fun, I enjoy traveling, watching game shows, reading, digital art, and spending time with friends. Personal interests include photography, current events, and volunteering.
