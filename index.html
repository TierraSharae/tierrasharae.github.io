About Me

About Me

Hello, world! I am a recent first-generation college graduate from Omaha, Nebraska, where I studied journalism and media communication. I worked at a local television news station for nearly two years before pursuing a master's degree in computer and information technology at the University of Pennsylvania.

Fields of interests include UI/UX design and research, front-end development, developer relations, technical writing, product management, and communication.

For fun, I enjoy traveling, watching game shows, reading, and spending time with friends. Personal interests include photography, current events, volunteering, diversity in STEM, and poetry.
