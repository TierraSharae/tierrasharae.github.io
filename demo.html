Pastime Pixels

Mondrian, Landing Page

I created a landing page for Mondrian, a fictional subscription-based artwork service. The theme was inspired by dutch painter Piet Mondrian's 'Composition II in Red, Blue, and Yellow.'

#DailyUI: Credit Card Checkout

For Day 2 of DailyUI's 100-day Design Challenge, I created this mockup. Designing the credit cards was super fun!

I shot the product images with a Canon EOS 800D and edited them with Photoshop.
