Relevart

Relevart is a digital, community scrapbook for travelers to share photos. I chose this name, because it includes the word "art" and when reversed, spells "traveler," as shown in the logo. I aimed to draw the letters in such a way that the text would be legible when horizontally flipped; this was a very fun challenge!
