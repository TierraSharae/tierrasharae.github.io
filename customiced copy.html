    Your hands are unique -- why isn't your mouse? Customiced is a mouse created by you for you. Unlike traditional mice, Customiced fits your hand’s size, shape, grip preference, comfort, and style.

    As a project for IPD 515: Product Design at the University of Pennsylvania, I worked with two other graduate students to design Customiced. We explored the product design process from top to bottom, analyzing customer needs, interviewing prospective users, prototyping, testing, and more prototyping. Additionally, we analyzed specifications, ergonomics, and design for manufacturing as well as finances, sustainability, design for the environment, patentability, naming, and marketing. For a more in-depth look at each aspect, check out our executive summary. On July 3, my teammates and I presented Customiced at Penn's Summer 2019 Product Design Fair.

    As a personal project, I am currently working on an interactive, responsive website for Customiced.
